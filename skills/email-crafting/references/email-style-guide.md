# Email Style Guide

Preferred first email:

- 120-160 words
- Plain text friendly
- One website-based personalization point
- One product relevance point
- One light CTA
- No large attachments

Avoid:

- "best price"
- "guaranteed"
- "buy now"
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Long company history

Good CTA examples:

- "Would a short catalog and price range be useful?"
- "May I send the specification sheet for your review?"
- "Is this category handled by your sourcing team?"
